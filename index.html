Privacy Policy — Silver Japanese: Vocabulary

Last Updated: January 07, 2026

Quick summary: Silver Japanese uses a public word list (ManyThings), includes AI-generated definitions and example sentences, bundles pronunciation audio generated with LuvVoice, displays personalized ads, and uses Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) SDK to collect and manage consent for users in the EU/EEA and the UK. The app is not directed to children.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the application “Silver Japanese: Vocabulary” (the “app”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app. By using the app you agree to the terms described here.

2. Information We Collect

Information stored locally on your device

  • Language preferences (UI language, display options) — stored on your device so the app remembers your choices.
  • Practice progress and session data — stored locally so you can resume studying where you left off.

Information shared with third parties

When ads are displayed the app works with advertising partners. Those partners may collect or receive:

  • Advertising identifiers (for example, Android Advertising ID)
  • Ad interaction data (ad views, clicks)
  • Device information (OS version, device model)
  • IP address and approximate location (used for ad delivery and fraud prevention)

The app does not require you to create an account and does not, by default, collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other direct identifiers.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain app functionality.
  • Remember language preferences and study progress.
  • Serve advertisements, including personalized ads.
  • Improve app stability and the user experience.

4. Advertisements, Personalization & Consent

The app displays personalized advertisements through Google AdMob.

EU/EEA/UK users — UMP SDK

For users in the EU/EEA and the UK, the app uses Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) SDK to present a consent form and record your choice for ad personalization.

  • The consent form lets you accept ads.
  • Your choice is respected and applied to ad requests.
  • Ads are personalized by default, but if those are not possible due to an error, the app will display non-personalized ads.
  • If you explicitly decline ad storage/consent required by some ad providers, any kind of ads will not show at all.
Note: ad networks have their own data practices. For details and global ad settings you can review Google’s Ads Settings and AdMob documentation.

5. Third-Party Services & Content Sources

  • Google AdMob — used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data as described above. See Google’s privacy documentation for details.
  • Content sources:
    • The words used in the app are built upon the word list from ManyThings: manythings.org.
    • Definitions and example sentences were created with the assistance of AI tools.
    • Pronunciation audio included with the app was generated using LuvVoice.

6. Data Security

Local preferences and practice data are stored on your device, and we do not recieve personal data from the user.

7. Data Retention

  • Local app data remains on your device until you clear the app’s storage or uninstall the app.
  • Third-party services (AdMob) retain data according to their own retention policies. Please review those services’ privacy policies for details.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location you may have rights under applicable privacy laws (for example, GDPR in the EU/EEA/UK) including the rights to:

  • access personal data;
  • request correction or deletion;
  • restrict or object to certain processing (including ad personalization); and
  • data portability, where applicable.

To exercise these rights or ask questions about your data, contact us at the address below. We will respond in accordance with applicable law.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove that information.

10. International Transfers

Third-party vendors (ad networks, AI providers, audio providers) may process data in countries outside your own. Where required by law, appropriate safeguards should be used. For details consult the vendor privacy policies or contact us.
